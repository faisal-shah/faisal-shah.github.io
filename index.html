The Institute

We are a not-for-profit Islamic educational institution based in Houston, TX. The institute is dedicated to providing authentic Islamic education to our community through qualified teachers. The philosophy of our courses is distinct in its embrace of the entire academic tradition of the Ahl al-Sunnah wal-Jama’a with respect for all scholars of Tafsir, Hadith, Fiqh, and Usul al-fiqh.

The Mission

Our mission is to help our students develop strong Islamic identities through love for Allah swt, Prophet sws and good company (sohbah), while providing them a thorough understanding of their religion that is both intellectually and spiritually uplifting. To that end, along with in person classes (as permitted due to the COVID pandemic), we also offer live online classes to cater to a geographically diverse audience and ensure the safety of all participants.

Who are we

Sameera Institute is managed and was founded by Ustaadhah Sameera Shah. Sameera was born and raised in Lahore and graduated with a B.Sc (Honors) in Economics from LUMS. She received her Alimiyyah degree in 2009 from Wifaq ul Madaris Board of Pakistan, and certifications in Advanced Quran Study and Tafsir and Advanced Islamic Jurisprudence from Whitethread Institute UK. Sameera is a homeschooling mom of three energetic boys. She loves teaching introductory and advanced level courses on Quran, Tafsir, Hadith, Islamic Law, Arabic grammar, and Purification of the heart to young girls and women.

Mariam Sattar was raised in Houston, Texas where she received her B.Sc. in Biochemistry from the University of Houston. She began her pursuit of classical Islamic learning at Zaynab Academy Online studying Quran, Tafsir, Hadith, Fiqh, Usul al-Fiqh, and Arabic under traditional scholars, and has completed her Alima Degree. Mariam taught at Sameera Institute for four years teaching tafseer, fiqh, usool Fiqh, and Arabic. Mariam also delivered talks on purification of the heart and drawing closer to Allah (swt). She is now a student at Qalam Institute where she is revisiting some subjects and learning from a contemporary standpoint.

Rukaiya Ahamed was raised in Houston. She graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Nutrition from the University of Houston and is currently a Certified Surgical Technologist. She began her journey learning the deen with the Classical Islamic Knowledge program at Sameera Institute in 2015. She studied Tafsir, Hadith, Fiqh, and Arabic under Ustazah Sameera Shah and Ustaadh Mariam Sattar. She has been a Teacher’s Assistant and teaches courses part time at Sameera Institute. She loves working with the youth. She coordinates and teaches Girls Youth Programs through Sameera Institute at different masajid in Houston.

She then completed a full time Seminary program from Qalam Institute and is now furthering her studies as a full time year 3 Alimiyyah student at Qalam institute. She teaches courses part time at Sameera Institute to kids, youth and adults.
